WHOSE JOB IS IT
Whose job is it
Once a system is installed and invoiced, you're responsible for collecting outstanding payment from finance customers specifically — cash customers are handled separately by Accounts. Work through this at least twice a week using the Collections Report, and never let an invoiced customer sit untouched for more than a few days without at least a status check.

A QUICK TIP THAT SAVES A LOT OF BACK-AND-FORTH
A quick tip that saves a lot of back-and-forth
WHY IT MATTERS
If a customer has made a partial payment, resend the invoice with the updated balance rather than manually explaining the new figure over email or phone. Updating the invoice date and saving automatically triggers a new email to the customer — let the system do the explaining.
