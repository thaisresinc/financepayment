WHY THIS ROLE EXISTS
Why this role exists
The finance team sits between three other teams: sales (who close the deal), the finance companies (who fund it), and fulfillment (who install it). Your job is to keep every order moving through that chain correctly — valid finance details, prompt approvals and referrals, and a customer who's kept in the loop the whole way. Nothing in this course is optional colour — every module covers something that will land in your inbox in your first few weeks.
